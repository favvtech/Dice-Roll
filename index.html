🎲 Game Rules

Game Rules:

  • Roll dice to build your current score
  • Hold to add current score to total
  • Roll a 1 and face penalties based on difficulty setting
  • Risk increases with consecutive rolls (difficulty feature)
  • First to reach target score wins!

Difficulty Settings:

  • Win Score: Set your target score (100-1000)
  • Dice Count: Choose 1 or 2 dice
  • Snake Eyes Penalty: Lose Turn, Lose Score, or Both

Power-ups:

  • 🛡️ Safe Roll: Can't lose score on 1 (2 uses per player)
